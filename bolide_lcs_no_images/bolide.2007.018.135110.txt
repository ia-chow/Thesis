the flash signature of a large meteoroid entry into the atmosphere. Location of the
flash  was Lat 5.3N, Lon 164.3W.

was  determined to be approximately 1.8E10 watts/steradian, and total radiated flash
energy  3.2E10 joules (6000K blackbody model).
