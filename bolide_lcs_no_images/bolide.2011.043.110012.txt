Bolide Detection Notification: 2011-043

At 11:00:12 UT on 12 Feb 2011, sensors aboard U. S. Government satellites recorded
the flash signature of a large meteoroid entry into the atmosphere. Location of the
flash  was Lat 7.8N, Lon 95.3W.

Based upon the visible-light intensity-time signature, peak brightness of the flash
was  determined to be approximately 9.3E9 watts/steradian, and total radiated flash
energy  2.0E10 joules (6000K blackbody model).

plots  below. On the intensity-time plot, one count on vertical axis corresponds to
